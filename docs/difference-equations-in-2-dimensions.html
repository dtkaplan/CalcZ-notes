38 Difference equations in 2 dimensions

  1. [Dyn-3a] Learn how to construct future states of a dynamical system in two dimensions given the dynamical rule and the two-dimensional initial condition.
  2. [Dyn-3b] Appreciate that global dynamics can be complicated, potentially with many fixed points, learn how to identify the locations of the fixed points.
  3. [Dyn-3c] Understand how local dynamics near a fixed point are comparatively simple and can be meaningfully approximated by a matrix.
  4. [Dyn-3d] Review the mechanics of matrix arithmetic and iterate a dynamical system written in terms of a matrix.
