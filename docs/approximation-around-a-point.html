Q-148: What order approximation in graph (IV)?

  1. constant []

  2. linear [The contours would be straight if the approximation were linear]

  3. bilinear [Not a bad answer. But curvature in bilinear approximations is always in one direction.]

  4. quadratic (+) [Sometimes quadratic approximations produce elliptical contours, as in a previous problem. But sometimes they produce the X-shaped contours seen here. In both cases, the contours curve in opposing ways in different parts of the domain. By the way, the contour pattern seen in the upper right of this graph corresponds to the shape of a saddle: curving up along one line and down along the perpendicular line. The place right in the middle of the saddle is called a ‘saddle point’.]
